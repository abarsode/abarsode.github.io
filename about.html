Hi! I am Alok, a UX/front-end developer based in London, UK. I am passionate about technology, open source and building exciting products. I have worked extensively on Linux and have contributed to open source projects like BlueZ and ConnMan.If you find my Résumé interesting, send me an email and I will get back to you as soon as possible. Thanks! Here is a what I have been upto in the past few years...

I graduated from college with a computer science degree in 2005 and started my career writing device drivers for RTlinux. I added support for 3COM's 3c905 and Intel's e1000 NICs and added scatter-gather support in the driver. I continued working on different projects until 2008, when I started working on the linux bluetooth subsystem, Bluez, and soon caught the open source bug !

I contributed my first patchs for the HFP bluetooth profile and got invited to the 2008 BlueZ developer conference. The conference gave me great exposure to open source and convinced me that this is what I should continue doing! Over the next couple of years I continued contributing to Bluez. In 2010 I took part in the Google summer of Code as a mentor for Bluez. I also gave talks about the bluetooth stack at tech conferences.

In the winter of 2010, I decided to move to Helsinki to work more closely with the BlueZ folks. In early 2011 I started working on ConnMan, a free and open source networking stack. The project was in its infancy and I got a chance to contribute to a project early on, shaping it and seeing it grow. I continued to work on core ConnMan until 2012, when I decided to move to London.

London was a UX hub for the company, and it got me interested in UX. I developed and maintained GNOME UIs for ConnMan. I also developed UI for oFono, the open source telephony stack. I soon moved to front end programming and have not looked back since ! I have done concept applications to demos, using emerging standards like Polymer and tools like CCA. More on this in my Résumé.

When I am not programming, I am either listening to music, trying to take pictures with my DSLR, travelling or playing with my cat :). I love to play squash/badminton and watch tennis on the telly!
